## Who is this for?

This guide is for data-savvy marketers, CRM managers, and Technical Account Managers who want to go beyond basic campaign setup. You should already be comfortable with the [Product Guide](/product-guide/introduction) fundamentals.

**Prerequisites:** Familiarity with data concepts (schemas, field types, filters), boolean logic, and the Reelevant platform UI.
