The Reelevant platform has been completely redesigned with a fresh, modern interface available at `next.reelevant.com`. Each application — Workflows, Contents, DataHub, Analytics, and Account — was migrated independently to ensure a smooth transition with no disruption to your campaigns.

<Note>
The new interface is primarily a **visual migration**. Unless a feature is explicitly listed on this page, the change is purely cosmetic — all your inputs, buttons, and workflows remain in the same place and work the same way. You do not need to relearn the platform.
</Note>

This page lists every **new feature and improvement** introduced alongside the redesign.

## Platform-Wide Features

These changes apply across all applications in the platform.

<Note>
As a reminder, if a screen or feature you use is not listed here, it means it was migrated as-is — same layout, same buttons, same behavior, just with a refreshed look.
</Note>

### Theme / Color Mode

*December 2025*

Customize the platform's appearance to match your preference with three color modes:

- **Light** — the classic bright interface
- **Dark** — easier on the eyes during long working sessions
- **System** — automatically follows your computer's light/dark setting

Switch themes instantly from the sidebar menu — no need to go to settings. Your preference is saved to your profile and stays consistent across all your devices and browser tabs.

### Folders in Lists

*March 2026*

All listing pages — Workflows, Contents, and Datasources — now support **folder organization**. This feature is exclusive to the new interface.

- Create folders and sub-folders to organize your items by campaign, client, team, or any structure that fits your workflow
- Navigate between folders using the breadcrumb bar at the top of the listing, with quick dropdown access to sibling folders
- Assign items to folders when creating or editing them, and create new folders on the fly

This is a game-changer for teams managing large numbers of workflows, contents, or datasources — no more scrolling through flat lists to find what you need.

</Frame>

### Settings Pages

*March 2026*

Three new settings pages are now built directly into the platform, replacing the previous external configuration tools:

- **Workflows → Settings: Variable Profiles** — create and manage reusable URL parameter sets for your workflow integrations. This is especially useful if you manage multiple brands or clients, each with their own tracking parameters — set up a profile once and reuse it across workflows

- **Contents → Settings: Custom Fonts** — upload and manage your brand fonts directly in the platform. These fonts are available in the Content Editor and are automatically matched when importing designs from Figma or Photoshop

- **Analytics → Settings: Attribution Period** — configure how many days after a campaign touchpoint a conversion should be attributed to that campaign. This directly affects your conversion reporting, so you can align it with your business's attribution model

## Workflows

The Workflows application was the first to receive the new interface. All existing features (node configuration, triggers, conditions, splits, channels) **work exactly the same way** — the migration is visual only. The features listed below are genuinely new additions.

### Redesigned Workflow Editor

*September 2025*

The Workflow Editor got a complete visual refresh, making it faster and more intuitive to build your automation flows.

What's improved:
- **Cleaner node configuration** — setting up conditions, splits, and data nodes is now done through organized slide-out panels instead of crowded menus
- **Better workflow listing** — find your workflows faster with improved search, sorting by last update, and folder organization (see [Folders in Lists](#folders-in-lists))
- **Shareable views** — filters and editor settings are saved in the URL, so you can share a specific view with a colleague by simply copying the link

### Branch Qualification from Connections

*March 2026*

Qualifying branches is now much faster. Instead of digging through the logic node menu, simply **hover over any connection line** in your workflow and click **"Qualify branch"** to open the qualification panel.

This makes it easy to:
- **Name your branches** so they're recognizable in reports (e.g. "VIP Customers", "New Subscribers")
- **Assign categories** from your nomenclature to structure your analytics by business segment
- **See at a glance** which connections belong to the branch you're editing — they're highlighted on the canvas while the panel is open

You can still zoom and pan the canvas while the panel is open, so you never lose context.

### Undo / Redo

*April 2026*

Made a mistake? Just press **Ctrl+Z** to undo and **Ctrl+Shift+Z** to redo. This works across both the Workflow Editor and the Content Editor, covering all types of changes — moving nodes, editing text, adjusting layout guides, and more. No more fear of accidentally breaking something.

## Content Editor

The Content Editor received a full visual redesign. All your existing tools — text editing, image placement, data binding, conditions — **remain in the same place and behave the same way**. The features below are new capabilities added on top of the migration.

### Content Editor Revamp

*April 2026*

The Content Editor has been fully redesigned for a smoother creative workflow:

- **Resizable panels** — adjust the size of the configuration panel and layers tree to match your screen and preferences
- **Modular editing** — each element type (text, image, barcode, map, countdown, etc.) has its own focused configuration, reducing clutter
- **Instant preview** — see your changes reflected in real time as you edit, so you always know exactly what your content will look like

### Content Preview in Listing

*January 2026*

You can now see a **visual thumbnail** of each content directly in the listing page. No more opening contents one by one to find the right one — browse your entire library at a glance.

### Canvas Background Color

*May 2026*

You can now set a **custom background color** for the content editor canvas. This is purely a design aid — it helps you preview how your content will look against different backgrounds (e.g. a dark email template) without affecting the final output. The background automatically adapts when you switch between light and dark mode.

  <img src="/images/new-ui/content-canvas-background.png" alt="Canvas background color configuration" />

### Layout Guides

*May 2026*

Layout guides help you **align elements precisely** by overlaying visual grids on your content. This is especially useful for maintaining consistent spacing across product recommendation templates or multi-column layouts.

You can add three types of guides:
- **Grid** — a uniform square grid to snap elements into place
- **Columns** — vertical columns with customizable count, spacing, and margins (great for product grids)
- **Rows** — horizontal rows for aligning elements vertically

Each guide can be shown/hidden, renamed, recolored, and customized individually. You can have multiple guides active at once.

**Where to find it:** Click the grid icon in the toolbar at the bottom of the canvas.

### Datasource Element Picker

*May 2026*

When building product recommendation templates, you often need to display the same field (e.g. product name, price, image) for **multiple products**. The new element picker lets you choose which product position each field refers to — product #1, #2, #3, and so on, up to 20.

- When you insert a field that's already in use, a dialog asks whether you want to reuse the same product or pick a different one
- A badge on each variable shows which element it refers to (e.g. "element #2")
- You can change the element assignment at any time from the variable settings

This makes building multi-product layouts much faster and less error-prone.

## DataHub

The DataHub application was migrated to the new interface with the same navigation, data source creation wizard, and field configuration you're used to. **Everything works the same way** — the improvements below are new additions to help you work more efficiently.

### Redesigned DataHub Interface

*February 2026*

The DataHub received a cleaner, more efficient interface to help you manage your data sources with less friction:

- **Cleaner logs** — the "missing fields" noise has been moved to a dedicated configuration step, so your logs page only shows what matters
- **Last update column** — instantly see when each datasource was last refreshed, making it easy to spot stale data
- **Live status indicators** — a loading spinner appears next to each datasource name when an update is running, so you always know what's in progress
- **Simplified catalog** — creating a new datasource is more straightforward with a streamlined template picker
- **Expandable log details** — click on any warning or error to see the full details, making troubleshooting faster
- **Folder organization** — organize your datasources into folders, just like workflows and contents

## Analytics

The Analytics application was migrated to the new interface. Your existing dashboards, saved queries, and reports **are all still accessible in the same way**. The features below are new capabilities added alongside the visual refresh.

### Statistics Exploration Page

*December 2025*

The new data exploration page gives you a powerful **query builder** to run ad-hoc analyses on your campaign data. Select your workflows, campaigns, measures, dimensions, and time period — then view the results instantly.

Your query parameters are saved in the URL, so you can **bookmark** a specific analysis or **share it** with a colleague by sending them the link. You can also filter by branch category if you've set up nomenclatures for your workflows.

### Analytics Dashboards Revamp

*January 2026*

All Analytics dashboards have been redesigned with new capabilities to help you understand your campaign performance better:

- **Comparison period** — see how your metrics compare to a previous period (e.g. this week vs. last week) to quickly spot trends
- **Temporal granularity** — switch between daily, weekly, or monthly views to analyze performance at the right level of detail
- **Data export** — export data from any KPI card, chart, or table for use in your own reports and presentations
- **Branch category filtering** — focus your analysis on specific business segments by filtering dashboards by your branch categories

### Usage Dashboard (Consumption)

*January 2026*

Keep track of your account's consumption with a dedicated dashboard that shows how much of your contractual quota you've used. The dashboard includes:

- **Consumption overview** — a clear summary showing your total usage (openings or unique openers, depending on your plan) against your quota, with color-coded status: green when under quota, yellow when approaching the limit, and red when significantly over
- **Breakdown by team** — see which teams are driving the most consumption
- **Breakdown by channel** — understand consumption across email, web, mobile, and landing page channels

Accessible via **Analytics → Consumption** or **Account → Consumption**.
