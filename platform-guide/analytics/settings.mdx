## Overview

The Analytics Settings page lets you configure global analytics parameters for your company. Access it from the **Settings** link in the Analytics sidebar navigation.

## Attribution Period

The attribution period defines the number of hours used to attribute conversions to a [workflow](/platform-guide/workflows/overview) interaction. When a user clicks on Reelevant content and later makes a purchase within this time window, the conversion is credited to that content interaction.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Attribution period (hours)** | The lookback window in hours (e.g., 720 for 30 days). Leave empty to use the default attribution period. |

### Configuring the Attribution Period

1. Navigate to **Analytics > Settings**.
2. Enter the desired number of hours in the **Attribution period (hours)** field.
3. Click **Save**.

<Info>
  Changing the attribution period affects how conversions are counted in the [Conversions dashboard](/platform-guide/analytics/dashboards/conversions) and [Data Exploration](/platform-guide/analytics/data-exploration) queries. A longer period captures more conversions but may include less directly attributable purchases.
</Info>
