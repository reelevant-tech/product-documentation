## Overview

The Usage dashboard tracks your openings consumption during the subscription period. It helps you monitor whether your usage is within the planned quota.

## Usage Overview

The consumption card shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Subscription period** | Your subscription runs from the start date to the end date. |
| **Plan type** | Whether the plan counts the **number of openings** or the **number of unique openers**. |
| **Planned usage** | Your subscription quota. |
| **Current consumption** | How much of the quota has been used. |

### Consumption Status

A banner indicates your consumption status:

| Status | Message |
|--------|---------|
| **Success** (green) | Your consumption is within the planned quota. |
| **Warning** (yellow) | Your consumption has exceeded the planned quota. |
| **Error** (red) | Your consumption has largely exceeded the planned quota. |

## Usage Tables

Two breakdown tables provide detailed consumption data:

| Table | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Usage by team** | Consumption broken down by resource group (team). |
| **Usage by channel** | Consumption broken down by [channel](/platform-guide/workflows/channels) type. |

<Tip>
  Use the team breakdown to identify which teams are driving the most consumption. This helps with capacity planning and budget allocation.
</Tip>
