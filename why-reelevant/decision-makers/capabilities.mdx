## The Problem

Your CRM team sends millions of emails, but every customer sees the same Content. Segmentation helps, but it still groups thousands of people into buckets. The result: generic messages, declining engagement, and no way to prove ROI beyond open rates.

Meanwhile, your competitors are moving toward individual-level personalisation — and your customers expect it.

## What Reelevant Does

Reelevant makes every email individually personalised **at the moment of open** — not at send time. Each customer sees Content selected specifically for them based on their real-time context: lifecycle stage, purchase history, browsing behaviour, loyalty status, and preferences.

| Without Reelevant | With Reelevant |
|-------------------|----------------|
| Same banner for all customers | Each customer sees their own lifecycle message |
| Static product recommendations (batch, 24h old) | Real-time recommendations based on latest browsing + stock |
| No way to measure personalisation ROI | Built-in incrementality measurement (Exposed vs Non-Exposed) |
| 6 segments × 4 campaigns = 24 versions to maintain | 1 Workflow handles all variants automatically |

## Core Capabilities

### Real-Time Content Decisioning

Workflows — the automation logic that decides what to show each person — evaluate conditions at the moment of email open: "Is this customer a one-timer? Show second-purchase incentive. Is their loyalty status about to expire? Show urgency message."

**Outcome:** Your CRM team builds one Workflow that handles 50+ customer scenarios automatically. No more duplicating campaigns for each segment.

### Personalised Product Recommendations

Each customer sees products selected for them based on purchase history, browsing, preferences, and real-time stock. Unlike batch recommendation engines, products are selected at the moment of open — reflecting what happened since the email was sent.

**Outcome:** Recommendation blocks that convert 3–5× better than static bestseller lists. Products are always in stock, in the right language, with applicable offers shown.

### Built-In Incrementality Measurement

Every Reelevant deployment includes a Non-Exposed Population (10%) that sees email without personalisation. This provides a rigorous, always-on measurement of the Incremental Value generated by personalisation.

**Outcome:** You can prove to your CFO that personalisation generated €X in incremental revenue — with statistical confidence, not estimation.

### Lifecycle Automation

Datasources — the data connections that feed customer information into Workflows — enable lifecycle-aware personalisation: welcome journeys, second-purchase nudges, dormant reactivation, VIP recognition, and churn prevention — all from a single Content block.

**Outcome:** Replace 20+ static campaign automations with 1–3 Workflows that adapt to every customer stage.

### Loyalty Programme Integration

Display real-time loyalty data within emails: points balance, tier status, benefits, and progression. Non-members see personalised enrolment incentives. Members close to the next tier see urgency messaging.

**Outcome:** +2.5 pts Uplift on loyalty enrolment rates. Members spend 30%+ more than non-members — each incremental enrolment is measurable value.

## How It Compares
