Your Reelevant plan is measured by how much your campaigns are consumed. This page explains what counts as consumption and how to follow it.

## What Counts as Consumption

Reelevant bills your usage on the number of opens your [Workflows](/product-guide/workflows/overview) generate. Depending on your plan, you are counted on openings or on openers.

| Term | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Opening** | Each time a Workflow displays Reelevant Content to someone. |
| **Opener** | A unique person who triggered at least one opening. |

<Tip>
  One opener can create many openings. If a person opens the same email three times, that is 1 opener and 3 openings.
</Tip>

Your plan counts one of these two:

| Plan type | How consumption is counted |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Openings plan** | Every opening adds to your consumption. |
| **Openers plan** | Only the first opening from each person counts. |

## Open the Usage Dashboard

You follow your consumption on the [Usage dashboard](/product-guide/analytics/dashboards/usage), a dedicated page in the Analytics app.

1. Open **Analytics** from the main navigation.
2. Select **Dashboards**.
3. Choose **Usage**.

## Read Your Consumption

The consumption card at the top shows where you stand for the current period.

| Field | What it tells you |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Subscription period** | The start and end dates your plan covers. |
| **Plan type** | Whether you are counted on openings or on unique openers. |
| **Planned usage** | The amount included in your plan. |
| **Current consumption** | How much you have used so far. |

A coloured banner tells you how close you are to your plan limit.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Green** | Your consumption is within the planned amount. |
| **Yellow** | You have gone over the planned amount. |
| **Red** | You have gone well over the planned amount. |

<Warning>
  Going over your planned usage can affect your billing. Check the banner regularly so there are no surprises.
</Warning>

## Break Down Your Consumption

Two tables under the card show where your consumption comes from. Each row shows one value, so nothing is grouped together.

| Table | What it shows |
|-------|---------------|
| **Usage by team** | Consumption split by [Team](/product-guide/account/teams). A Workflow shared by two Teams appears once per Team. |
| **Usage by channel** | Consumption split by [Channel](/product-guide/workflows/channels) type. |

<Tip>
  Use the team breakdown to see which Teams drive the most consumption. This helps you plan capacity and share budget fairly.
</Tip>
