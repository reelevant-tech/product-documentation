## The 8 Steps of a Rigorous Incremental Measurement

_This page will contain the full methodology translated from source material. Content to be completed by CS + TAM teams._

### Steps Overview

1. Ask a business question
2. Identify the relevant population
3. Constitute the Exposed and Non-Exposed Populations
4. Verify that both populations are comparable
5. Choose the observation duration
6. Calculate the Uplift
7. Translate Uplift into revenue
8. Document, interpret, and reproduce
