The Base Engagement category addresses challenges around maximising the reachable audience, activating new channel users, and leveraging feedback data to personalise communications.

## Key Challenges in this Category

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Extend CRM Consents" icon="envelope-open">
Encouraging contacts to opt in on multiple channels (Email, Push, RCS, WhatsApp) to maximise CRM reach and relational value. A multi-channel contact often generates significantly more value than a mono-channel one.

**Connected Use Cases:** 2
</Card>

<Card title="App Adoption & Activation" icon="mobile-screen">
Converting app downloaders into regular active users. The app is the richest channel in behavioural data and the most direct for real-time personalisation.

**Connected Use Cases:** 1
</Card>

<Card title="Leveraging NPS for Personalised Communication" icon="face-smile">
Using the NPS score and product reviews to differentiate communications based on individual satisfaction. A promoter and a detractor should not receive the same message.

**Connected Use Cases:** 2
</Card>

</CardGroup>
