## Key Principles

### Incremental Value, not captured value

Most personalisation tools count all conversions from users who saw personalised content. Problem: most of those conversions would have happened anyway. That's **captured value**, not **created value**.

Reelevant measures only what would NOT have existed without personalisation — by comparing an Exposed Population (90%) against a Non-Exposed Population (10%) within the same campaign.
